ABOUT

I am a R&D engineer specialized in the numerical modeling of physical and chemical processes. I'm currently a Research Associate at the University of Clermont Auvergne.

My education and previous professional experiences provided me with a robust expertise in the development and use of numerical simulations. I use various numerical methods like computational fluid dynamics or the discrete element method to support innovative projects in research and development.

For more details about my skills, have a look at the skills section. Please feel free to contact me at alexandre.carrara.rd@gmail.com
