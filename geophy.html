Satellite radar interferometry

Measurement of earth's surface deformation using satellite radar images. Development of a new method to measure 3D displacement field on lava flows.

Measurements of surface deformation on Colima volcano, Mexico, using radar interferometry.

The physical model for the cooling and thermal contraction of a lava flow on a volcanic flank.

Heat transfers and cooling models

Numerical modeling of the cooling and contraction of a solid material in contact with air. Application to the cooling of lava flows. The numerical model employs the Finite Element Method (FEM) and was implemented using Matlab.

Propagation of elastic fractures

Numerical simulation of the formation of elastic cracks in solids as a function of external stress. Application to the modeling of dikes (fracture filed with magma). The simulations were performed using the software COMSOL.

Trajectory of fractures as a function of the external loading (Pload) and fluid pressure within the crack (Pm).
