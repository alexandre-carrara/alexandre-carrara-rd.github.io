Non-spherical Discrete Element Method

Simulation of the settling of non-spherical particles using the software LMGC90.

Settling of a mixture of elongated cuboids and dodecahedrons. The simulation uses cyclical boundary conditions.

CFD-DEM with non-spherical particles

Simulation of the coupled flow of liquid and solids accounting for non-spherical particles. The simulations are performed using the software MigFlow for the fluid phase and the software LMGC90 to model the granular phase.

Simulation of the shearing of a dense suspension composed of non-spherical particles. The color of the particles depends on their velocity. The motion of the particles is entrained by the fluid flow. The ambient liquid is transparent.

Simulation of gravity currents

Simulation of granular gravity currents using the discrete element method. Application to landslides. The simulations were performed using the software MFIX-DEM.

Simulation of a landslide. The particles that form the landslide are displayed in white. The ones colored in gray correspond to the topography.
